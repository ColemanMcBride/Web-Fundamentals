Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to build revenue by attracting potential customers, relaying important information about pricing and creating an expectation of a wonderful experience with our company.

Audience

Our main audience is parents of families with teen/young adult aged children, meaning we will focus our content will be on family fun and long-lasting memories. These parents are likely trying to expose their children to positive experiences and bond as a family. I imagine that our customers will approach our website from many different platforms (desktops, laptops, and mobile devices), so trying to make a website that is simple and flexible would likely be optimal.
